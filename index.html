What is BookShare?

BookShare is a community-driven platform designed to connect book enthusiasts. Our mission is simple: reduce book waste and increase access to literature by enabling people to share books they've already enjoyed with others who want to read them.

Whether you have a stack of novels gathering dust or you're looking for your next great read, BookShare makes it easy to give and receive books for free.
