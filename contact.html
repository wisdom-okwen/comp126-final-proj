Frequently Asked Questions

How do I request a book?

Simply visit our Request page, fill out the form with your details and the book you want, and submit. We'll check availability and get back to you!

Is there a cost to borrow books?

No! BookShare is completely free. We're a community-driven platform focused on sharing knowledge.

How long can I keep a book?

Generally, we ask that you return or pass on books within 30 days so others can enjoy them too.

Can I donate books?

Absolutely! We welcome book donations. Contact us with details about the books you'd like to share.
