Frequently Asked Questions

How do I request a book?

Visit the Request page, fill out the form with your details and the book you want, and submit. I'll check my collection and get back to you!

Is there a cost to get books?

No! All books from my collection are completely free, including shipping.

Do I need to return the books?

Nope! Once I send you a book, it's yours to keep. Enjoy it and maybe pass it on when you're done!

What if a book I want isn't available?

If a book is currently unavailable, you can still submit a request and I'll notify you if it becomes available.
